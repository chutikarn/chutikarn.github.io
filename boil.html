SOFT BOIL

Jammy Yolk. Soft Egg Whites. Ultimately the Perfect Boil! These are traditionally found in ramen. The eggs are paired with shoyu broth and topped alongside Chashu(pork). But of course, feel free to put a little spin on these eggs by adding them on top of salad or rice.
