CRISPY FRY

That Perfect Crispy Edges. Fried in Shimmering Olive Oil, not Butter. Repeat: Olive Oil Sunnysides cooked on butter? Noo, that's not how you enjoy eggs, I believe. And I promise, the crispness that surrounds the molten yolk will be a great start to your day.


TECHNIQUE

  • Make sure water is at a gentle simmer
  • Crack 1 egg through a sieve set over a bowl
  • Stir to create a gentle vortex in saucepan
  • Tip egg into center of vortex
  • Continue to swirl gently with spoon
  • Make sure the white sets around the yolk
  • Cook until white sets and lift out with slotted spoon
  • Serve! Season with salt and pepper!
